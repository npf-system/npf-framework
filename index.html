NPF

New Physical Forms

NPF is an artistic research framework focused on the development of new physical forms through autonomous systems, material processes, and non-linear temporal structures.

Projects function as test sites rather than resolved objects.

Lab

BIG SLURP
Ongoing investigation · Proposed installation

BIG SLURP explores continuous inversion as a spatial condition. A textile body of constant length is turned through itself by a distributed system, producing a persistent loop without origin or resolution.

The project treats inversion not as an event, but as a sustained state in which inside and outside remain temporary and reversible.

Based in Sweden
